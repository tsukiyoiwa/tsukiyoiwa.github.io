Cancellation Policy

At Tsukiyoiwa, we have encountered situations where guests make multiple reservations at different restaurants, but fail to show up on the reserved date without any prior notice. We find this situation regrettable.

Like many other restaurants, we carefully prepare all ingredients based on the number of reservations for the day and reserve seating arrangements and service staff accordingly.

Therefore, we kindly request all guests to read and understand the following deposit and cancellation policy before making a reservation. Your cooperation is greatly appreciated.

Regarding Reservation

To uphold our customers' reservation rights, Tsukiyoiwa requires a deposit to be prepaid during a specific period when making a reservation. Failure to receive your deposit payment by the specified date will result in automatic forfeiture of the reservation, and the seat will be made available to other guests seeking to reserve.

If you need to cancel or modify your reservation, please ensure to contact us by phone within the specified time.

About Deposit

A deposit of NTD3,000 will be charged per person.

About Reservation Cancellation and Changes in Number of Guests

Changes in Number of Guests:
We accept slight changes in the number of guests until 17:00, three business days before the reservation date. After that, we will no longer accept changes in the number of guests.

Example: If you have made a reservation for a 8200 NTD set menu for 3 guests, but on the day of the reservation, only 2 guests arrive without any prior notification, we will still provide the meal for 3 guests and charge for 3 guests' portions.

Date modification or cancellation

No change or cancellation of the reservation date will be accepted for reservations of 1~4 or more people, including the first three days of the reservation date; for reservations of 5 or more people, including the first five days of the reservation date (excluding Sundays), no refund of the deposit will be made.

If you need to modify or cancel your reservation, please call us before 17:00 three days in advance (for 1~4 people) or five days in advance (for 5 people or more).

For example, if you want to modify the dining date from September 1 to September 3, it will be considered a cancellation, and no deposit will be reimbursed. (Reservation for 1~4 people) For example, on September 1st, a date modification or cancellation for the initial meal reservation of September 6th will be approved. (Reservation for groups of more than 5 people)

*Please note that once the reservation for the special requirements set menu is approved, the amount of people, dates, or cancellations cannot be modified, or the deposit reimbursed.
